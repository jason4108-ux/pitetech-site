Who We Are

R&D, production, sales and support from one Shenzhen manufacturer.

PITE TECH INC, Chinese name 深圳市普禄科智能检测设备有限公司, was established in 2001 with registered capital of 13.29 million yuan. The company is a national high-tech enterprise with core technology, patents, software and hardware development, production and sales capabilities.

PITE started with portable intelligent detection instruments. As industrial IoT, artificial intelligence and big-data platforms matured, the company expanded toward full-life battery safety management and distributed energy management service platforms.
