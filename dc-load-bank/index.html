Features

Designed for practical field maintenance.

Each feature is organized around technician workflow: setup, measurement, protection, data transfer and reporting.

  • Programmable constant-current discharge keeps the load stable through the test.
  • Automatic shutdown by time, total string voltage, capacity or individual cell voltage.
  • Wireless communication supports safer remote monitoring of live discharge tests.
  • Parallel operation with another unit increases current capacity when needed.
  • Built-in circuit breakers support emergency shutdown and overload protection.
  • Optional DAC package records individual cell voltage and exports detailed reports.
