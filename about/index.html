About PITE

A trusted brand in intelligent detection equipment since 2001.

PITE TECH INC develops and manufactures testing instruments for batteries, DC power supplies, AC power quality and railway maintenance applications.

Who We Are

R&D, production and support under one manufacturer.

PITE TECH INC, Chinese name 深圳市普禄科智能检测设备有限公司, was founded in 2001 with registered capital of 13.29 million yuan. The company focuses on intelligent detection equipment, hardware and software development, production and sales.

From portable field instruments to IoT-enabled detection platforms, PITE aims to help customers manage battery and power assets through the full lifecycle.
